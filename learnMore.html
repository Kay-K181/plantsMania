💡 Fun Facts

Plants can “hear” themselves being eaten

Some plants release defensive chemicals when they detect the sound of insects munching on their leaves. 👂🐛

Plants can “talk” to each other

When under attack, plants release chemical signals that warn their neighbors to defend themselves. 💬🌿

Some seeds can survive for thousands of years

The Judean date palm seed sprouted after 2,000 years! 🌰⏳

Trees can “sniff” the wind

Certain trees detect airborne chemical signals from other plants and react to threats in advance. 👃🍃
